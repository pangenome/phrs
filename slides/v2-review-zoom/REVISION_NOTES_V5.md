## Interpretation Boundaries

- The `07j` workflow slide should be used to avoid implying that each arm-pair
  matrix cell comes from a separate graph. The PGGB graph is built once over all
  extracted PHR paths, and arm-level cells are averages over path-pair Jaccard
  values.
- Slide `07j.1` is a main-component ODGI layout-coordinate view, not a full
  graph overview and not a new graph layout. It uses component `8` from the
  existing `.og.lay.tsv`; no heavy ODGI extraction, layout, or edge-level redraw
  was submitted.
- Same-arm `A x A` cells are same-arm self-bundle averages, not forced identity
  values. They can be below 1 because they average distinct haplotypes and graph
  paths from the same chromosome arm.
- Use "copy-aware support" or "copy-number-aware candidate signal" for the v5
  gene-enrichment section.
- The plotted v5 enrichment evidence is HPRCv2 arm/community support plus the
  OR4F copy gradient. It is not a production genome-wide copy-weighted ORA.
- Do not present parked weighted-ORA p=0 rows, large fold-enrichment values, or
  uncalibrated FDR behavior as final statistics.
- Do not imply every community-assigned arm has a called CHM13 PHR interval in
  `chm13.phrs.bed`. The caveat arms are C5 `chr6_p`, C7 `chr13_p`, C14
  `chrY_q`, and C15 `chrY_p`.
- The `07a.1` and `07a.2` heatmaps use the same similarity matrix, same
  heatmap palette, and same fixed Jaccard similarity scale; only the order and
  tree/community annotations change.
