## What Changed From v4

- Replaced the single v4 slide `07a` UPGMA heatmap/tree asset with two
  sequential v5 slides:
  - `07a.1`: tree/UPGMA-ordered 41-arm similarity heatmap with the side tree.
  - `07a.2`: the same 41-arm matrix sorted by Leiden C1-C15 community, with no
    side tree and with community bands and diagonal community boxes.
- Kept the heatmap story local to the sequence-community block. Existing `07b`
  and `07c` tree backup slides are retained after the new pair.
- Revised slide `14m` into a copy-number-aware enrichment method transition.
  The slide explicitly separates community-arm support from called CHM13 PHR
  interval status and names the community-assigned arms without called
  `chm13.phrs.bed` rows: C5 `chr6_p`, C7 `chr13_p`, C14 `chrY_q`, and C15
  `chrY_p`.
- Added three copy-number-aware enrichment figure slides:
  - `14a`: method boundary for standard ORA, deduplicated ORA, HPRCv2
    copy-aware support, and parked genome-wide copy-weighted ORA.
  - `14b`: ranked copy-aware candidate signals, plotted as support counts rather
    than q-values.
  - `14c`: community/family support map with the CHM13 interval caveat shown in
    the slide asset.
- Relabeled the existing v4 gene-browser panels as backup slides `14d` through
  `14h`; their image assets and source provenance are unchanged.
- Updated deck metadata and default footer text from v4 to v5.

This is not a broad redesign. The changes are limited to the v5 heatmap pair,
the gene-enrichment section, slide labels around that insertion, and the render
artifacts.

## Interpretation Boundaries

- Use "copy-aware support" or "copy-number-aware candidate signal" for the v5
  gene-enrichment section.
- The plotted v5 enrichment evidence is HPRCv2 arm/community support plus the
  OR4F copy gradient. It is not a production genome-wide copy-weighted ORA.
- Do not present parked weighted-ORA p=0 rows, large fold-enrichment values, or
  uncalibrated FDR behavior as final statistics.
- Do not imply every community-assigned arm has a called CHM13 PHR interval in
  `chm13.phrs.bed`. The caveat arms are C5 `chr6_p`, C7 `chr13_p`, C14
  `chrY_q`, and C15 `chrY_p`.
- The `07a.1` and `07a.2` heatmaps use the same similarity matrix, same
  heatmap palette, and same fixed Jaccard similarity scale; only the order and
  tree/community annotations change.
