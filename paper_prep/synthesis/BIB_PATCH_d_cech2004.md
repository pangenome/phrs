## Abstract

A comprehensive review article covering the discovery of telomerase by Greider and Blackburn (1985) and subsequent advances in understanding both the RNA and protein subunits of this ribonucleoprotein enzyme across diverse organisms (ciliates, yeast, humans). Discusses telomerase regulation at the level of enzyme synthesis and substrate (telomere) state, with emphasis on roles in cellular immortality and cancer.
