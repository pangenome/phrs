## Abstract excerpt (cancer-relevance)

> "We report that DUX4, an early embryonic transcription factor that is normally silenced in somatic tissues, is re-expressed in diverse solid cancers. … DUX4 expression blocks interferon-γ-mediated induction of MHC class I, implicating suppressed antigen presentation in DUX4-mediated immune evasion. … Our results demonstrate that cancers can escape immune surveillance by reactivating a normal developmental pathway."
