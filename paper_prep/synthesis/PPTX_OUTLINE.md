## Slide 38: All-vs-all is insane… but…

**Layout:** TITLE_AND_BODY+figure

**Body:**
- The all-to-all alignment of 465 × 3 billion bp implies a 2 septillion (2×1024) cell matrix.
- But most of the cells in that matrix are empty (it’s sparse). Your typical whole genome aligner exploits this.
- Panmixia means that most of the possible pairwise alignments are redundant. A random sample is enough.
- we avoid quadratic “pollution”
