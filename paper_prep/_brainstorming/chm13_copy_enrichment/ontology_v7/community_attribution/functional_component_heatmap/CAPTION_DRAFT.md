# Candidate Extended Data Caption

**Copy-number-aware functional component burden across chromosome ends.**
Heatmap of coordinate-distinct CHM13 PHR physical copies carrying each
post-inference V7 display class. Columns are chromosome ends; the community
ordered version groups the 41 signal-bearing arms by arm-level PHR community
C1-C15 and orders arms within each community by the subtelomeric similarity tree,
with the seven no-signal arms appended in grey. Cell values are physical PHR copy
counts, not collapsed gene identities and not redundant GO/Reactome term rows.
Display classes summarize exact primary-supported ontology rows after V7
inference and do not constitute additional enrichment tests. The pattern shows a
mosaic organization: DUX4/ZGA signal is concentrated on the C1 4q/10q system,
whereas WASH/DDX11, SEPTIN14, WBP1L and RPL23A components recur across selected
communities, consistent with partially overlapping subtelomeric cassettes rather
than wholesale homogenization of chromosome ends.

The companion `exact_supported_term_arm_matrix.tsv.gz` contains all 209 exact
primary-supported V7 ontology rows. Those rows reduce to 66 distinct
chromosome-end copy patterns, supporting use of the six display classes as the
readable figure layer.
