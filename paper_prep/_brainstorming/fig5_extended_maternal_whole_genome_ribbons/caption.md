# Source Caption: Maternal Fig. 5 Panels

The maternal whole-genome homologous-competition ribbon views are incorporated
into the manuscript as Fig. 5C-D. `PAN027mat_vs_PAN010_joint` compares the
PAN027 maternal haplotype with the two PAN010 mother haplotypes, and
`PAN028mat_vs_PAN027_joint` compares the PAN028 maternal haplotype with the two
PAN027 mother haplotypes. In both panels, chromosomes are concatenated in
native order and scaled by assembled length. The middle track is the meiotic
product inherited by the child/query haplotype, and the upper and lower tracks
are the two parental haplotypes being compared. Colored ribbons mark
non-homologous class-winning runs; light-gray ribbons show the corresponding
same-chromosome homologous background.

The detailed SweepGA 10:10 pre-filter, IMPG class-winner procedure, depth
filters and grouping thresholds are documented in `METHOD_NOTES.md` and in the
manuscript Methods, not repeated in the figure caption.
