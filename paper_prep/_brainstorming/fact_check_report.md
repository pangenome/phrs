### Patterns of LLM Fabrication

#### **Type 1: Data Misattribution**
- Correct numerical values attributed to wrong genes/processes
- Mixing up enrichment signals between different gene families
- **Example**: 825-fold enrichment exists but for IQSEC3, not TUBB8

#### **Type 2: Citation Fabrication**
- Missing references for specific clinical claims
- Very detailed statistics without verifiable sources
- **Example**: "Chinese populations 3-5%" with no citation

#### **Type 3: Biological Implausibility**  
- Claims that contradict basic biology
- **Example**: Olfactory receptors expressing in brain regions

#### **Type 4: Non-existent Entity Claims**
- Making claims about genes/proteins not in the dataset
- **Example**: OR4F4 brain expression when gene not in analysis
