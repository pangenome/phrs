## Overall Grade

`0.88 / 1.00`

This is a strong completion of the task. The actor corrected the endpoint
semantics in the merge routine, made the rule auditable, regenerated the figure
package, and pushed the work. The generated evidence convincingly shows that
multi-window drawn runs now span their merged query intervals, and the donor
intervals in the output are also expanded rather than first-window-only.

The main reason this is not a 0.95+ score is the validation asymmetry:
`validate_run_spans()` enforces query-span correctness against `bp`, but donor
span correctness is only indirectly evidenced through output columns and audit
maxima. A stronger implementation would add explicit donor-span or
donor-first-window-only assertions and possibly a tiny fixture/regression test
covering forward and reverse donor chains.
