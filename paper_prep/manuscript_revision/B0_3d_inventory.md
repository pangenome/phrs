# B0 3D Contact Evidence and Source Inventory

Date: 2026-06-17

Scope: human 3D contact evidence for manuscript Cluster B. This is a source audit only: no
figure rebuilds, no Slurm jobs, and no heavy recomputation. The goal is to make the
lead evidence separable from defensive controls for B1/B4/B5.

## Reading Guide

Classification:

- **Lead evidence**: clean, direct evidence that can carry the main text or a figure.
- **Support**: useful corroboration, but not the cleanest first-line evidence.
- **Defensive apparatus**: controls or robustness checks that answer reviewer concerns.
- **Caveat / negative control**: evidence that limits or qualifies the claim.

The current manuscript's cleanest human lead evidence is the pointwise
community-free Spearman analysis: each dot is an inter-chromosomal PHR sequence pair
within one genome, comparing PHR Jaccard to length-normalized 3D contact at the
pair's coordinates. The flanking unique-sequence results are the cleanest
multi-mapping defense and should stay visible, but they are controls, not the
primary biological measurement.
